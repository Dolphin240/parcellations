Binary masks used in this study

# gunzip *.nii.gz files before running MATLAB code. 

GMmask.nii.gz: a binary mask for the whole brain gray matter

subcortex_mask.nii.gz: a binary mask for the entire subcortex including thalamus, hippocampus, amygdala, caudate, nucleus accumbens, putamen and globus pallidus 

subcortex_mask_part1.nii.gz: a binary mask comprising thalamus, hippocampus and amygdala.
